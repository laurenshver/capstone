Who Are We?

We are a team of undergraduate students in Business Technology Management at Ryerson University. Each of us brought a unique perspective and an array of skills that were critical in the success of our capstone project. In our final year, we remotely balanced heavy workloads during the COVID-19 pandemic and worked tenaciously to bring our project to fruition.

This capstone project is based on a fictitious company, ATSR, that requested a redesign of their current system to account for the growth in their client base and provide a competitive advantage in service. Throughout this website, our proof-of-concept includes functionality for ATSR’s staff members to: view all tools in the system; create and view quotes, orders, customer accounts; and process orders.
